About the Author

Sondra J. Hardy

Sondra J. Hardy, born and raised in West Haven, Connecticut, is a writer whose work traces the hidden tensions within families and communities. A graduate of West Haven High School and Bay State College, where she earned the Beatrice Ruderman Award, Hardy brings a historian’s eye and a storyteller’s patience to her narratives. Her literary work, influenced by authors like Walter Mosley, explores themes of secrecy, betrayal, and faith across interconnected urban landscapes.
